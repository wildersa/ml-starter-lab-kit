# Clustering Metrics

Use clustering metrics when there is no target and the model creates groups.

## Inertia

Measures how close samples are to their cluster centers.

Useful for K-Means, but not enough alone.

## Silhouette score

Measures how similar a sample is to its own cluster compared to other clusters.

Higher is usually better.

## Practical warning

A good clustering metric does not automatically mean the clusters are useful for business.

Always interpret the clusters.
